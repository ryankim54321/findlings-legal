Frequently asked

How do I identify a plant?

Tap Scan, point your camera at a plant (or choose a photo from your library), and Findlings identifies it in seconds with a confidence rating. Every find is saved to your collection.

What's included with Findlings Pro?

Pro unlocks unlimited identifications, rich species facts, CSV/PDF export, batch identification, and your shareable Year in Review. Free accounts get 10 identifications a day. Pro is $2.99/month or $19.99/year.

How do I restore a purchase or cancel?

To restore, open the paywall and tap Restore Purchases. To cancel, go to Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone and turn off Findlings Pro. Subscriptions renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the period ends.

Where is my data stored?

Your collection lives in your own iCloud and syncs privately across your devices. There's no account to create and no social feed — it's just for you. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Is a plant safe for my pets?

Findlings flags plants that are toxic to pets right on the identification — for free, always. When in doubt, contact your veterinarian.
