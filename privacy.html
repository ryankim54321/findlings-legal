Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Findlings is built privacy-first. There's no account to create, no advertising, and no analytics or tracking of any kind. This policy explains the little data the app does handle and where it goes.

The short version: Your photos, finds, notes, and the locations you attach to them live in your own iCloud — we never see them and have no server that stores them. When you identify a plant, only the photo (with no location attached) is sent to a botanical identification service to recognize it. We don't track you, show ads, or sell anything to anyone.

What the app handles, and why

  • Photos you identify. When you scan or choose a plant photo, that image is sent to the Pl@ntNet identification service to return a species match. The photo is re-encoded before sending, which removes any embedded location (GPS) and camera metadata. We don't receive or store these photos ourselves.
  • Your collection. Your discoveries, photos, journal entries, moods, and progress are stored in your private iCloud account (Apple CloudKit) and sync across your own devices. This data is controlled by you through Apple; Findlings has no backend server and no copy of it.
  • Location. If you allow it, the app records where you made a discovery so it can show your finds on a map. This location is stored only in your own iCloud — it is never sent to us and never sent with the photo to the identification service. You can decline location access and the app still works.
  • Purchases. Findlings Pro is sold through Apple's In-App Purchase. Apple processes payment; we never see your payment details.

What we don't do

  • No user accounts, logins, emails, or passwords collected.
  • No advertising and no ad networks.
  • No analytics, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs, and no "Data Used to Track You."
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of personal data — there's nothing collected to sell.

Third-party services

Two services are involved in the app's core functions:

  • Pl@ntNet — receives a plant photo to identify it. See Pl@ntNet's terms and privacy information.
  • Apple iCloud & In-App Purchase — stores your collection privately and processes subscriptions. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

Findlings is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages, but it is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your control and deletion

Because your data lives in your own iCloud, you're in control: delete any find inside the app, or remove the app's data through Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage on your device. Deleting the app removes its local data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above and post the new version here. Material changes will be reflected in an app update where appropriate.
